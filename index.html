Introduction

DELTA Diving & Marine Services was founded in Aqaba- Jordan in 2014, Since then, DELTA has become one of the most powerful companies’ providers of divers and significant solutions that related with marine works and services,. provide modern, innovative and sustainable solutions to our client in marine sector, Safety is number one objective for our work and the environment. DELTA provides quality services in one of the most competitive global industries in order to maximize client’s results, Personnel, equipment and our operations are of the highest standards.

Our Commitment to

Quality, health, safety, and environment. DELTA only employs highly trained HSSE officers. Our HSSE Engineer is IOSH and NEBOSH certified. Our goal is to have Zero accident or LTI. DELTA prides itself on operational excellence, which can be directly attributed to our highly competent and diverse workforce, both on and offshore. Hard to ensure we're recruiting the right person for the job and through our individual performance management processes evaluates and develops the capability and competence of our people. This includes a successful leadership development.

Our PEOPLE

We value our employees, not just for what they do, but for the way they do it, and we demonstrate our appreciation through our reward and recognition schemes. We have more than 20 divers and employees are highly trained and experienced from multiple main projects in Jordan and Saudi Arabia. They are also certified commercial, rescue, and PADI divers. Our core values of Gender Equality, excellence, innovation, integrity, leadership, respect, and teamwork underpin the way we work together to provide our clients with efficient and safe operational services.
